Popix (Popular Pix) is a mobile photo sharing application.

Demo: http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=TworTztXB5s

Authors: Richard Brookfield & Jason Campbell

Welcome to POPIX "Popular Pictures".

POPIX is a picture sharing application that allows users to send pictures to all other active users. Users can vote on pictures and explore their friends pictures.

Built on top of NodeJS, Phonegap and JQM
